# World Map & Knowledge

## Zones

### Miden'nir Forest
- **Danger Level**: Above recommended level for newbies
- **Starting Location**: The Miden'nir (forest room with goblin corpse)
- **Monsters**: Mountain goblins, goblin lieutenant, wyverns
- **Navigation**: Dense forest, easy to get lost, many dead ends
- **Route to Midgaard**: n, n, n, n (going north repeatedly through forest)

- Southwest corner connects to South Gate
- South Gate leads to Miden'nir forest trail

**Park Road**:
- Runs north-south through eastern part of city
- Cityguard Headquarters: West from Park Road (locked)
- Park entrance areas

**Promenade**:
- Runs along the river on north side
- White benches for resting

**Other Streets**:
- Emerald Avenue: Runs north-south, connects to Road Crossing
- Penny Lane: East from Emerald Avenue, dead end at fountain
- Elm Street: East from Park Road, dead end at elm tree

**Location**: Main Street, south from middle section

### Guild of Swordsmen
**Location**: Main Street, far east (south from main street that passes weapon shop)
**NPCs**: Knight guarding entrance, Guildmaster (in Tournament and Practice Yard)
**Guildmaster Location**: Tournament and Practice Yard (south from Bar of Swordsmen)
**Description**: Guild for fighter classes. Contains entrance hall, bar, and practice yard
**Services**: Skill practice (kick available for level 1+)
